Our Story

Engineering Excellence in Fibre-Reinforced Plastics

OM POLO FIBRE PLAST was founded with a single mission — to provide industry with FRP / GRP equipment that lasts longer, resists corrosion better and performs reliably under demanding conditions.

Over the years we have grown into a trusted manufacturing partner for power plants, paper mills and process industries, delivering everything from precision fittings to large fabricated systems. Our combination of skilled craftsmanship, quality materials and engineering know-how sets us apart.

  • Experienced in-house design & fabrication team
  • Premium resins & reinforcement materials
  • End-to-end project execution & support
